What is Mölkky Kanto Prime League?

Starting in 2023, Japan will be implementing a league called #Mölkky Prime League. #mkpl (unofficial and not affiliated with JMA)

The Prime League will feature a maximum of 8 Mölkky club teams competing in a round-robin tournament over 7 months to determine the champion.

Previous Mölkky tournaments were often one-day events where the winner was decided based on that day's performance and luck, but the new league format will better reflect the true abilities of players.

Participating in the Prime League will also provide players with more opportunities to gain match experience, which will allow for more effective training for larger domestic and international tournaments.

Additionally, some games will be streamed on YouTube, bringing attention to individual players and their personalities and playstyles, potentially creating "star players" and raising the overall level of Japan through the sharing of tactics and throwing techniques.

Season 1 is set to begin in April and clubs and players are currently being scouted.

We hope to raise awareness of the League among Mölkky players around the world and draw attention to the Mölkky scene in Japan.
